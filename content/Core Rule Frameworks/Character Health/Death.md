If you lose all 5 Health Levels you are Incapacitated and Dying. 

At the end of the scene you will make a Death Check - the base difficulty is 13, and you’ll roll the greater between Spirit or Vitality.

- On a Fumble, lose your 6th health level. 
- On a Miss, you’re still unstable. The next time you would roll for Daily Recovery instead make a new Death Check. 
- On a Hit, you stabilize. You're still incapacitated & don’t regain any lost Health Levels yet, but this trial won’t be the one to lay you low. 
- On a Triumph, benefit from the effects of a Hit plus you gain Advantage on your next Daily Recovery Check.

If you lose a 6th health level, you die. Your soul will visit the Hall of Journeys, you will have clarity about your life and potential, and there you may return home as you pass through the doors of eternity. The harsh conditions that have affected the world and the struggles of survival have also altered magic itself. Since the displacement of the gods, revival magic has fled from the world.

If you did not die but also did not stabilize, [[Medical Aid]] and [[Health Track#Starred Conditions|starred conditions]] can help with future Death Checks like normal.
