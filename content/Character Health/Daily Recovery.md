The world is striving to heal itself - and that includes you. Each dawn you make a Vitality Check for daily recovery with a difficulty of 13. In certain areas of the world the difficulty of this check may be greater or lesser than normal.

- On a Hit, restore one lost Health Level. 
- On a Triumph restore 2. 

If dawn occurs during an encounter, the GM will have you make this check either right before or right after.

[[Medical Aid]] and [[Health Track#Starred Conditions|starred conditions]] can help you recover more quickly.
