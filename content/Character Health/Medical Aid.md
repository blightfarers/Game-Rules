A caretaker or physician may make checks, with time and necessary resources, to tend to the needs of the suffering. 

When a caretaker has basic medial supplies or can improvise them and gives medical aid to another character, the patient clears any leftover tallied damage that did not meet the Wound Threshold (and therefore, did not become a lost Health Level).

While a caretaker may not directly restore one of your lost Health Levels they can provide long term monitoring and care that improves your chances of recovery.

When a caretaker provides care specific to their patients necessities by explicitly treating the ailment, cleaning wounds, monitors them, and re-dresses wounds or re-applies treatment as needed over the course of hours or more they may make a Medicine Check.

- On a Hit, the patient gains a +2 Asset on their next Daily Recovery, Sickness, and Death Checks. 
- On a Triumph they gain Advantage in addition to the +2.

 The base difficulty for such Checks is at least 13. Special effects & circumstances may modify the difficulty of checks for healing like normal.

Tools and time are almost always required, although some resources may be improvised. Plus, filth and infection love to get in the way when they can.
