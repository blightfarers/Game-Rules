# 1. Assign Attribute Points
- Spend 10 upgrade points to increase the value of chosen Attributes
	- From 0 to 1, costs 1 (total 1)
	- From 1 to 2, costs 1 (total 2)
	- From 2 to 3, cost 2 (total 4)
	- From 3 to 4, cost 2 (total 6)
	- From 4 to 5, cost 4 (total 10)

Upgrading a stat beyond 5 is not allowed for starting characters.
# 2. Allott Background Ranks
- Choose one Background, assign 1 to 3 ranks to it
- Assign any leftover ranks to a 2nd or 3rd Background
	- You may also spend one of your Background points to instead gain 2 Talent points. Then assign these as Ranks in appropriate Talents of your choosing.
# 3. Select Lineage Traits
- Select 3 traits representative of your character's lineage, affecting form, capability, and nature. Any of these can be swapped for an additional archetype trait.
# 4. Choose Archetype Traits
- Select 2 traits from available archetype classes. Either can be swapped for an additional lineage trait.
# 5. Equip Starting Gear
- Choose from common gear options:
	- 1 armor set
	- 3 wieldable items (swords, shields, etc)
	- Tools someone of your Background would have
	- Rations, money, torches.
